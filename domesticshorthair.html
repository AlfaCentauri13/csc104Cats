Domestic Shorthair

Fun Facts about Domestic Shorthair Cats

  • Domestic Shorthairs come in a wide variety of colors and patterns, making them one of the most diverse cat breeds.
  • They are known for their easy-going nature and adaptability to different environments.
  • Many Domestic Shorthairs are highly intelligent and can be trained to perform tricks or use a litter box.
  • They are often good with children and other pets, making them great family companions.

General Facts about Domestic Shorthair Cats

Lifespan:
12-15 years
Diet:
Balanced diet with high-quality protein sources. They generally have fewer dietary restrictions compared to purebred cats.
Behavior:
Domestic Shorthairs are known for their friendly and adaptable behavior. They are usually active and enjoy playtime but also appreciate a good nap. They are low-maintenance compared to long-haired breeds.
