Classic Orange Tabby

Fun Facts about Orange Tabby Cats

  • Orange Tabby cats are not a specific breed but rather a color pattern found in many breeds.
  • They are known for their friendly and affectionate nature, which is why they are a very popular breed of cat.
  • Their distinctive orange color is due to a genetic variation affecting pigmentation.
  • Many Orange Tabby cats have a distinctive "M" shape on their foreheads!
  • Classic Orange Tabby cats have bold swirling patterns on their backs like marbling in a cake.
  • They also have a random series of dark and light swirls like a bullseye.

General Facts about Classic Orange Tabby Cats

Lifespan:
12-15 years
Diet:
High-quality cat food with a balanced diet of proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.
Behavior:
Playful, social, and affectionate. They often enjoy interacting with people and other pets.
